# Week 2: AI Meeting Minutes Extraction Assistant

### 1. Business Workflow Definition
* **Workflow:** Transform messy raw meeting notes or call transcripts into structured to-do lists.
* **User:** Project Managers or Team Leads.
* **Input:** Raw meeting text containing dialogue from multiple speakers and heavy use of colloquial language.
* **Output:** A structured Markdown document including:
    * **Meeting Overview:** Concise summary of the discussion.
    * **Key Decisions:** Major conclusions reached during the session.
    * **Action Items:** Specific tasks with assigned owners and deadlines.
* **Value:** Automates tedious organizational work, ensures no team tasks are overlooked, and improves post-meeting execution.
